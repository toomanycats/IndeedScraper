Keywordcounter Sample Output

This bar charts are samples of keywordcounter.com's outputs for a given job search on indeed.com by title. In this case, digital marketing.

The first you'll see is an analysis of keyword pairs. The program searches the job postings for bullet points and gathers all the text in those points. Then the text is analyzed for common word pairs. Pairs which occur too often or not few times, are not considered.

This analysis is similar to the one above, except the words are not pairs but single words. Also, the words have been reduced to their "stems" before analysis. This is helpful, because now words like:

  • work
  • worked
  • working

    • Are grouped as the same word.

    It's often helpful to know which job titles were found, especially if you are searching by keyword rather than title.

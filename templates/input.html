Finding Keywords That Optimize Your Resume Or Social Media Content

Many advice articles agree that you should optimize your resume and other social media with branding keywords. How does one go about getting those ? Typically you spend several hours reading job postings. This tool will save you the time, by compiling 50 or more job postings and applying techniques to extract the relevant keywords and phrases.

Select whether you will search for job postings using the title of the job, or a keyword approach. If I wanted to know which industries are seeking applicants with Micro Soft Excel skills, then I might use keywords, "excel".

If you know the title of the positions you're seeking information about, such as, "director of marketing", use "director marketing" and a title search.
